About Sza

Sza is a popular R&B/Soul music artist in today's society. Her full name is Solána Imani Rowe. She was born in St.Louis, Missouri, and her birthday is November 8, 1989. Her newest and latest release is her new song, "I Hate You" which was released on December 3rd 2021. But she started releasing music back in October of 2012 with her debut EP called, "See.SZA.Run".
